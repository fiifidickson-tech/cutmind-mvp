How it works
From words to patterns in three steps.

CUTMIND doesn’t replace your factory or 3D software. It sits in between: translating your language into pattern edits you can plug into your existing workflow.

01
Pick a base block

Start with a vetted T-shirt block designed for real bodies, not just stock models. Long sleeve and cropped variations ship next.

One base block, infinite riffs, consistent fit.

02
Describe the fit in your words

“Vintage boxy tee.” “Snatched baby tee.” “Relaxed but not sloppy.” CUTMIND maps those phrases to measurable changes in the pattern.

No spreadsheets of measurements required.

03
Export and send to your maker

Download the adjusted pattern and bring it into your 3D tool or send it straight to your pattern-maker or manufacturer.

MVP = pattern edits only. Tech packs come later.

Why it matters
Built for founders who think in vibes, not millimeters.

If you know exactly how you want the garment to feel but hate talking in pattern language, CUTMIND is the translation layer between your brain and your block.

Translation engine
Your phrases → pattern rules

Predefined “ease bundles” turn words like cropped, boxy or oversized into a consistent set of edits, so your brand has a repeatable signature fit.

Example: “oversized” might add 4–6cm through the chest and 2–3cm through the sleeve.

Consistency
Lock in your base fit

Once you dial in a base block you love, CUTMIND keeps that logic as you crop, extend, or widen pieces – instead of reinventing the fit in every file.

Think “your brand’s tee” instead of random fit from factory to factory.

Speed
More ideas, fewer back-and-forths

Test multiple variations in one sitting and only send the factory the version you’re actually excited about.

You stay in creative mode; CUTMIND handles the nerdy edits.

Roadmap
Start narrow. Then expand.
  • Phase 1 – MVP
    Natural language → JSON rules → SVG edits for three base blocks: T-shirt, long sleeve, crop top.
  • Phase 2 – Library
    Hoodies, crewnecks, cargos and dresses. More ease bundles tuned for different aesthetics (Y2K, vintage boxy, slim).
  • Phase 3 – API & teams
    Plug CUTMIND into existing tools so bigger teams can keep their pipeline and just use the engine under the hood.
